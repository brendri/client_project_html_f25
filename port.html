Portfolio Hub

Portfolio Purpose

Take a moment to reflect on the purpose of your portfolio and what you hope to achieve with it.

Questions to ask yourself:

  • What skills and experiences do I want to showcase?
  • Who is my target audience (employers, clients, etc.)?
  • What types of projects best represent my abilities?

Research Portfolio

Include projects that demonstrate your research skills, such as:

  • Data and research papers you've written
  • Data analysis projects
  • Case studies

Design Portfolio

Showcase your design work, including:

  • Graphic design projects
  • Wireframes and prototypes mockups
  • User experience (UX) designs

For more information on Portfolios, check out the Portfolios for All Pathways guide.

Portfolio Guidelines

Portfolio Navigation

Your portfolio should be organized in a way that is easy for your audience to navigate. Consider the following structure:

  • 3+ Relevant Projects: A section that showcases your best work. Include detailed descriptions, your role in each project, and more.
  • About Me: This page is all about you. Include a professional photo and a brief bio that highlights your skills, interest, and what makes you unique.
  • Contact Information: Include your email address and links to your LinkedIn.
  • Resume: Have it embedded or one click to open as PDF in a separate tab.

Build your Branding

Take the time to think about your personal brand and how you want to present yourself in your portfolio. Keep your theme consistent across all projects.

Website Platform

They are number of platforms you can use to build your portfolio website:

  • Wix: A user-friendly platform with drag-and-drop features.
  • Squarespace: Sleek and modern templates.
  • Framer: A design-focused platform that allows for more control over the layout.

Check out more information on different available websites.

Project Layout

Identify these key components:

  • Project Summary: Role, client, duration, and problem/challenge.
  • Process: Show your process, including research, ideation, and design iterations.
  • Visuals: Include wireframes, storyboards, and any other relevant visuals.
  • Takeaways: Key learnings and insights gained from the project.

FAQ

  • What is the max projects to include in my portfolio?
    Try to keep it to 3-5 projects that best showcase your skills.
  • Can I use AI to help me?
    Yes, AI can be a tool in your portfolio development process, but ensure that the final product reflects your own skills and creativity. Check out the AI Prompt Resources guide for more information.
  • How often should I update my portfolio?
    It's a good idea to update your portfolio regularly, especially after completing new projects or gaining new skills.
  • Should I include a UX philosophy statement?
    A UX philosophy statement can help tell your design values and approach.
